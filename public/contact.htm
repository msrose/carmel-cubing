Q

I have joined the OLMC Rubik's Cube Club, and now everywhere I go I just can't seem to keep the ladies away from me! What do I do?

A

Getting all the ladies is a cross that all male members of the club must bear. The best remedy is to teach them how to cube! It gets their hands onto something other than you for a while.

Q

Are there any ladies actually in the club?

A

Yes, we have several avid cubers of the fairer sex.

Q

Do they get all the guys wherever they go?

A

Duh.

Q

Does the club provide me with my own cube?

A

You will not be provided with a personal cube, but there are extra cubes for members who do not own one. There are usually a variety of cubes for sale as well.

Q

Are there really snacks?

A

Of course. Feel free to contribute to the fun yourself by bringing in food to share.

Q

Do I have to be Asian to be a good cuber?

A

No, although there is significant evidence to suggest it would help. However, the arguably top cuber in the world, Feliks Zemdegs, is Australian, so it seems that there is hope for caucasians.

Q

I think I may be addicted to cubing. I can't seem to stop and it's distracting me from my everyday work. I cube so much, it may actually be a little off putting. What should I do?

A

How to tell if you're a cubing addict: 1. Anything you look at other than a puzzle is blurry. 2. You have trouble focusing on anything in the distance. 3. You realize you've been cubing for 6+ hours and forgot to eat or drink. If you meet all of these criteria, there is no hope. You will probably die with a cube in your hand in the next month. Can I have your swarm of ladies?

Q

I hear that there are hot Cuban boys at the club. Is this true?

A

I hate to disappoint, but none of the hot CUBING boys in the club happen to actually be Cuban.
