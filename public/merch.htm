How to Order

Follow these easy steps to reserve a cube for purchase.

Hide/Show
1

Fill out the fields marked "Name" and "Email" in the Your Cart section appropriately.

2

Go through the available items and select the ones you wish to purchase by checking the boxes marked "Add to Cart" in the top right corner of each item section.

3

Ensure that all the information found in the Your Cart section is accurate, and press order.

4

Your default mail program will be opened with an email addressed to Michael Rose. It will contain all the information from your order. Send the email with the subject "Cube Order".

5

You will be contacted within 48 hours about the final price of your order and when and where you can meet Michael to pick it up.

6

According to the instructions in the email, bring your money to the meeting with Michael and you will receive your merchandise.

7

Please note that only members of the club may purchase a cube.
