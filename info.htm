Goals

The OLMC Rubik's Cube Club has the main aspiration of spreading the joy of speedcubing throughout the school community. The club aims to teach all who join how to solve a 3x3 Rubik's Cube, other official WCA puzzles, and the endless collection of twisty puzzles that exist in modern cubing. Using the CFOP (Fridrich) method, club members can be helped to achieve average solve times of less than 15 seconds!

The secondary goal of the club is to hold competitions pitting OLMC's fastest cubers against each other, and give newer cubers a chance at competition simulated experience. This is all in preparation for competing in actual WCA competitions.

How to Join

Wondering how you can join the OLMC Rubik's Cube Club? It's easy: just show up! Meetings are very relaxed and informal: if you are a new cuber, there will certainly be someone willing to teach you how to solve. So come on out and see what we're all about! Keep in mind that complimentary snacks are available at most meetings! Please note that you must be a student at OLMC to become a member!

Lessons

Lessons are free, and there are always extra puzzles for those who do not own their own. However, it is recommended that you purchase your own cube if you plan on coming regularly - see our merchandise page for more details.
